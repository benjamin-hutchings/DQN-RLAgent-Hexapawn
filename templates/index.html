Hexapawn

How to Play

Hexapawn is a simplified version of chess played on a 3x3 grid. The game starts with pawns in the first row for both players.

  • Players take turns moving their pawns.
  • Pawns can move forward one square or capture diagonally.
  • The game ends when a player reaches the opposite side, captures all opponent's pawns, or no valid moves are left.
